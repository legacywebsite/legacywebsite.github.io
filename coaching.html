How to: Minecraft Edition In Minecraft, you have two options of gameplay survival or creativity and through this players explore a three-dimensional block world with virtually endless terrain where they can explore and mine raw materials, tools, craft items, and build constructions while processing earthworks and machinery all in First person perspective. According to Open Critic, Minecraft is rated a 9/10 for its “incredible constructions” and “grand adventures.” This game is fun for all levels of players whether you are new to games, experienced, or even a master. Minecraft provides an immersive experience and can even offer a challenge for those who are looking for one. The audience I plan to target is specifically noobies who are new to the game or even gaming in general. in the upcoming clips, I will be demonstrating the following techniques to help you get started on your adventure through the Minecraft world.  process

 

 1. Finding/ making a shelter is the first step because it is the most important step. When you first enter the Minecraft world you have about 15 minutes to create or find a sustainable shelter for you to live in This can be as simple as finding a cave or maybe even a village. However, building your own shelter could be as easy as digging a hole in the ground and remaining there until morning. 

 2. When you start in the Minecraft world the hunger bar is already pretty full meaning you can go quite a while before needing to find/ eat food. However, it is recommended to think ahead and find your source of food before that point. To do this you have to kill animals that are found roaming around the terrain 

 3.In Minecraft survival mode, a crafting table is essential because it allows you to create more complex items and tools. It gives you access to a larger crafting grid, which means you can combine different resources to make more advanced items. It's like having a bigger workspace to unleash your creativity! 

 4.Creating weapons is a crucial part of survival when it comes to survival mode in Minecraft. The zombies, endermans, and creepers are all enemies that are present within the terrain and they mainly come out at night so you will need to make weapons to defend yourself. 

 5. A light source is imperative when the sun goes down seeing as it gets really dark outside especially if you need to find food or shelter.
